ABOUT US

Sleep Tracker helps you notice the patterns in your sleep and find the best sleep schedule to optimize your mood.

The Importance of Sleep

Sleep is vital to our everyday functioning.

But it can be easy to let your sleep slip off track. Bright lights and bright screens can easily mess with our circadian rhythms, overriding the natural patterns that would normally tell us when to wake up and when to go to bed. Erratic sleep patterns, in turn, can lead to low energy, and an overall decrease in mood.

How We Can Help

Sleep Tracker calculates the optimum amount of sleep you should be getting, taking into account your self-reported mood and sleep patterns over several weeks.
